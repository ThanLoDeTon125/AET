fea 1: Aeterna begins with a journey of personal liberation, where players embody a time-traveling adventure, venturing into heritage lands recreated with precise 3D technology. The quest system is not mechanically pre-set but personalized based on behavior, allowing players to deeply interact with local cultural and historical values, drawing inspiration from folk values/games such as Muay Boran in Thailand, Sumpit & Wau in Malaysia, and more.

fea 2: The core of the game is its "Historical Personification" mechanism. Through the Gacha system, players have the opportunity to acquire characters based on the prototypes of heroes and historical figures from 11 ASEAN countries. Gacha is exclusively for Ancient Costumes (Skins) and Mythical Pets, allowing players to express identity without breaking game balance. Each character is a complete digital work of art with a distinctive design language, from ancient costumes and accompanying mythical creatures to skill sets inspired by historical anecdotes and a voice-over system with a strong local flavor.

fea 3: To avoid pure luck, Aeterna integrates "Cultural Accumulation Points." If players win an item, spirit, costume, or character, it's considered a stroke of luck. Each unsuccessful Gacha attempt is converted into valuable knowledge resources or items, ensuring players always receive commensurate knowledgeable and spiritual value. 

fea 4:Aeterna establishes a player-driven economy where rare items, skins, and characters become digital assets that can be traded and deposited. This blurs the lines between the virtual world and real value, transforming players' experience into tangible economic value and fostering a vibrant and lasting community. 

 fea 5:The game emphasizes synergy through team-based raids. Players not only conquer challenges together but also participate in missions with contemporary messages, such as eliminating "Pollution Monsters" and restoring green ecosystems.

 fea 6:The personal exhibition and community event features allow gamers to assert their individuality, transforming their heritage collections into a source of pride and a tool for connecting cultures across nations. 

 fea 7:Aeterna directly connects tourism and real-world consumption through its Hybrid Reward model. Owning rare in-game heritage collections unlocks real-life privileges such as service vouchers, attraction tickets, or tangible cultural artifacts. This is a complete value loop: Virtual Inspiration – Real Experience – Sustainable Value.
