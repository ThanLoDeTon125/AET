What is AETERNA?

Aeterna is a AAA Digital Heritage RPG Platform that utilizes Narrative AI to immortalize the soul of ASEAN. We transcend traditional gaming by merging Fair-play RPG mechanics with a Sustainable O2O Economy.

CORE VALUE: Honoring heritage through human-centric technology to foster shared prosperity and purposeful entertainment.

Vision

Becoming the world's leading digital heritage ecosystem, where all of humanity's cultural values are immortalized and brilliantly reborn in the Metaverse era.

Mission

  • Rejuvenating Heritage: Utilizing artificial intelligence to bridge the generational gap, transforming dry historical documents into captivating interactive experiences for young people.
  • Immortalizing Values: Digitizing and preserving the soul of ASEAN culture in the digital space, ensuring it never fades away over time.
  • Promoting Sustainable Economy: Creating a Hybrid (O2O) economic model that converts online users into real tourists, directly supporting local communities and heritage sites.

Core Features

Seven Pillars of AETERNA

Each feature is a gateway into a living heritage world — designed to preserve, connect and inspire.

Playing
01

Personalized Heritage Quests

Aeterna begins with a journey of personal liberation, where players embody a time-traveling adventure, venturing into heritage lands recreated with precise 3D technology. The quest system is personalized based on behavior, allowing players to deeply interact with local cultural and historical values, drawing inspiration from folk values such as Muay Boran in Thailand, Sumpit & Wau in Malaysia, and more.

Playing
02

Historical Personification

The core of the game is its "Historical Personification" mechanism. Through the Gacha system, players acquire characters based on heroes and historical figures from 11 ASEAN countries. Gacha is exclusively for Ancient Costumes and Mythical Pets. Each character is a complete digital work of art with distinctive design, from ancient costumes and mythical creatures to skill sets inspired by historical anecdotes and a voice-over system with strong local flavor.

Playing
03

Cultural Accumulation Points

To avoid pure luck, Aeterna integrates "Cultural Accumulation Points." Each unsuccessful Gacha attempt is converted into valuable knowledge resources or items, ensuring players always receive commensurate knowledgeable and spiritual value.

Playing
04

Player-Driven Economy

Aeterna establishes a player-driven economy where rare items, skins, and characters become digital assets that can be traded and deposited. This blurs the lines between the virtual world and real value, transforming players' experience into tangible economic value and fostering a vibrant and lasting community.

Playing
05

Team-Based Raids & Eco Missions

The game emphasizes synergy through team-based raids. Players conquer challenges together and participate in missions with contemporary messages, such as eliminating "Pollution Monsters" and restoring green ecosystems.

Playing
06

Personal Exhibition & Community Events

The personal exhibition and community event features allow gamers to assert their individuality, transforming their heritage collections into a source of pride and a tool for connecting cultures across nations.

Playing
07

Hybrid Reward — Virtual to Real

Aeterna directly connects tourism and real-world consumption through its Hybrid Reward model. Owning rare in-game heritage collections unlocks real-life privileges such as service vouchers, attraction tickets, or tangible cultural artifacts. A complete value loop: Virtual Inspiration – Real Experience – Sustainable Value.
